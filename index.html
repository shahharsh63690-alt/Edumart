About EduMart

EduMart was created with a simple mission: to make academic life more affordable and sustainable for students.

We provide a localized, safe, and community-driven platform for buying and selling textbooks, lab equipment, and other essential study materials. By keeping transactions within the campus community, we help students save money, reduce waste, and connect with their peers.
